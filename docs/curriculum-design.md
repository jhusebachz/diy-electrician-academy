# Curriculum Design

The curriculum follows a pre-apprenticeship progression:

1. Orientation and safety
2. Electrical fundamentals
3. Home electrical system anatomy
4. Tools and testers
5. Codes, permits, and documentation
6. Beginner observation and maintenance
7. Training board practice
8. Residential device theory
9. Troubleshooting mindset
10. Apprentice prep

Topics start with reading and conceptual learning, then move to visible observation, dead/unplugged training-board practice, and finally pro-only awareness. The app avoids presenting hazardous work as DIY.

## Resource Policy

Most YouTube entries are search links, not manually verified videos. They must be labeled as search links and paired with a safety disclaimer. Institutional links can point to allowed public safety organizations.

## Completion Policy

Checklist completion is used as a learning reflection gate. Marking a topic learned records local progress but does not certify competence.
