# Safety Model

DIY Electrician Academy is educational only. It does not replace a licensed electrician, local electrical code, permits, inspections, or apprenticeship instruction. Electrical work can cause shock, fire, injury, or death. If unsure, stop and call a licensed electrician.

## Topic Categories

- `learn`: reading, watching, and understanding concepts.
- `observe`: looking at visible home components without opening equipment or contacting wiring.
- `practice-board`: dead, unplugged, purpose-built training-board practice only.
- `pro-only-awareness`: conceptual awareness of work a licensed electrician should handle.

## Never Attempt as DIY

- Panel work
- Service entrance work
- Live electrical work
- New circuits
- EV charger installation
- Generator interlocks
- Subpanels
- Outdoor or wet-location circuits
- Aluminum wiring repair
- Knob-and-tube modifications
- Permit-required work
- Uncertain wiring
- Any task beyond the user's training, tools, permits, and legal authority

## App Controls

- Dangerous areas are labeled `pro-only-awareness`.
- Checklists favor learning, observation, and stopping conditions.
- Call-pro notes are included on every topic where uncertainty or hazards may arise.
- Search links are presented as learning resources, not verified instructions.
